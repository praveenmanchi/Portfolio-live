Title

In-flight entertainment System (Not part of TURKISH AIRLINES)

Objective

In-flight entertainment (IFE) is one of the more exciting features in long-haul air travel, with most airlines equipping seats with seat-back screens or displays. In-flight Entertainment (IFE) refers to the design and development of entertainment systems provided to airlines passengers. In-flight Entertainment Solutions can either be watching movies, entertainment channels, business, and news, listening to music or playing online games. In-flight entertainment solutions also facilitate and provide a wealth of information that could be of benefit to passengers, like, information on the travel route, onboard menu and procedural guidelines and other informative inputs.
